My Story

Hello! I'm Ruth Gezahegn, an aspiring front-end developer currently enrolled in the Nexus Front-End Boot Camp. My journey into web development began when I created my first simple webpage in high school, and I've been fascinated with the possibilities of the web ever since.

What excites me most about web development is the ability to create something from nothing and share it with the entire world. I believe that good web design should be both beautiful and functional, accessible to everyone regardless of their device or abilities.

When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking in the mountains, reading science fiction novels, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. I believe that diverse interests fuel creativity in unexpected ways.
